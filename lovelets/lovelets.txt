plan.md — Lovelet
1. Project Overview

Website Name: Lovelet
Purpose: A premium e-commerce platform selling all types of baby products including clothing, toys, skincare, feeding essentials, nursery furniture, travel items, and gift kits.
Target Audience: Parents, expecting mothers, families, and gift buyers.
Goal: Build a cute, elegant, and trusted one-stop baby shopping platform.
